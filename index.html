The Morgano Agency Inc — Independent Insurance Agency in Greenville, SC

The Morgano Agency Inc is an independent insurance agency located at 206B Pine Knoll Dr in Greenville, South Carolina. Founded in 1998 by Vic Morgano after 17 years with Prudential, the agency has served Greenville County and the Upstate for over 27 years from the same location behind Wade Hampton High School.

As an independent agency, The Morgano Agency is not captive to any single insurance carrier. The agency shops coverage across multiple carriers to find the right combination of price, coverage, and service for each client. Whether you need personal insurance for your home, car, and family, or commercial insurance for your Greenville business, the agency compares options so you do not have to.

Insurance Services

Personal Insurance in Greenville, SC

The Morgano Agency provides personal insurance coverage for individuals and families throughout Greenville County. Personal lines include auto insurance, homeowners insurance (HO-3 and HO-5 policies), renters insurance (HO-4), motorcycle insurance, boat and watercraft insurance, flood insurance through the NFIP and private carriers, umbrella insurance for excess liability, life insurance (term and whole life), and SR-22 filings for drivers who need to reinstate their license after a DUI or lapse in coverage.

Business Insurance in Greenville, SC

Commercial insurance from The Morgano Agency covers Greenville businesses of all sizes, from sole proprietors to companies with dozens of employees. Commercial lines include general liability insurance (CGL), workers compensation insurance required under SC Code 42-1-360, commercial auto insurance for fleet vehicles, business owners policy (BOP), contractor insurance with certificate of insurance (COI) issuance, professional liability (errors and omissions / E&O), cyber liability insurance, commercial property insurance, business interruption insurance, and commercial umbrella coverage.

Driving Directions to The Morgano Agency

From Downtown Greenville

Take North Main Street north. North Main becomes Wade Hampton Boulevard (US-29). Continue approximately 3.5 miles past the Cherrydale shopping area. Turn right onto Pine Knoll Dr. The office is in Suite B at 206, located behind Wade Hampton High School.

From Simpsonville and Five Forks

Take I-385 North toward Greenville. Take Exit 37 for Haywood Road. Turn left onto Haywood Road, continue to Wade Hampton Boulevard, and head north. Turn left on Pine Knoll Dr. Suite B is at 206.

From Greer and Taylors

Take Wade Hampton Boulevard (US-29) south toward Greenville. Pine Knoll Dr is on your left shortly after you pass Wade Hampton High School. Turn left and the office is at 206B.

From Travelers Rest

Take US-25 South to North Pleasantburg Drive. Follow Pleasantburg south to Wade Hampton Blvd. Turn right on Wade Hampton, then left on Pine Knoll Dr.

From I-85 (Spartanburg or Anderson Direction)

Take I-85 to Exit 51 for I-385 South toward Greenville. Follow I-385 to the Wade Hampton Blvd exit. Head north on Wade Hampton Blvd. Turn right on Pine Knoll Dr to 206B.
