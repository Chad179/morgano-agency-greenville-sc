The Morgano Agency Inc — Independent Insurance Agency in Greenville, SC

The Morgano Agency Inc is an independent insurance agency located at 206B Pine Knoll Dr in Greenville, South Carolina. Founded in 1998 by Vic Morgano after 17 years with Prudential, the agency has served Greenville County and the Upstate for over 27 years from the same location behind Wade Hampton High School.

As an independent agency, The Morgano Agency is not captive to any single insurance carrier. The agency shops coverage across multiple carriers to find the right combination of price, coverage, and service for each client. Whether you need personal insurance for your home, car, and family, or commercial insurance for your Greenville business, the agency compares options so you do not have to.
